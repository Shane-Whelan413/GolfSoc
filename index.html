Still running your league on
WhatsApp and spreadsheets?

Chasing scores by text. Updating handicaps by hand. Nobody knows where they stand until you post a photo of a spreadsheet on a Sunday evening. There's a better way.

Features
Everything your society needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Stableford scoring

Members submit their own scores from their phone. Points calculated automatically with stroke index per hole. No maths required.

Handicap tracking

Handicaps update automatically after every round. Admin verifies, publishes, and the leaderboard updates in real time.

Season leaderboard

Live standings updated after every event. Best 33 weeks count. Tied scores settled by backscore automatically.

Installs like a native app

No App Store needed. Members tap Share → Add to Home Screen and it lives on their phone like any other app. Works on iPhone and Android.

No spreadsheets needed

Scores, handicaps, fixtures and results all in one place. Admin controls everything from their phone. No laptop, no Sunday evening data entry.

How it works
Up and running in a day.
01

We set it up for you

Send us your members list, handicaps and course details. We configure the app with your society name and branding and hand you a link.

02

Members install it

Share the link with your society. One tap and it's on their home screen. No App Store, no accounts, no passwords to remember — just their PIN.

03

Play. Score. Repeat.

Members submit scores from the course. Admin verifies and publishes. Handicaps update and the leaderboard goes live — every Sunday.
