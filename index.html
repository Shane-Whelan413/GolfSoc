Stableford scoring

Members enter scores hole by hole. Points calculate live with handicap applied automatically.

Handicap tracking

Handicaps update automatically after every verified round. Full history kept per player.

Season leaderboard

Live standings with league points per round. Members can track their position all season.

Outings support

Full 18-hole outing scoring with front and back 9, best half tracking, and outing leaderboards.

Knockout competition

Full knockout draw management with match play scoring, playoffs, and automatic progression.

WhatsApp share cards

One tap generates a results image ready to share straight to your society WhatsApp group.

01

We set it up

02

Members log in

No app store needed. Members open the link on their phone, tap their name, enter a PIN — they're in.

03

Enter scores on the day

Each player enters their own score hole by hole. Points calculate live. Admin verifies at the end.

04

Share the results

Publish results to the leaderboard and share a results card to WhatsApp. Job done in minutes.
